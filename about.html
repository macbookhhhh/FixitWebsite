About Fixit

At Fixit, we are passionate about reducing waste and making a positive impact on the environment. Our platform connects people with surplus items, giving these goods a second life and ensuring that fewer resources go to waste. From food to household items, our mission is to make sustainability easy, affordable, and accessible for everyone.

Join us in our mission to save the planet one item at a time!
